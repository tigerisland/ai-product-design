
# Experiment 01: AI as a Design Thinking Partner

## Problem
Product designers often struggle to move from vague ideas to structured artifacts quickly, especially early in discovery.

## Hypothesis
Using Claude as a structured thinking partner can:
- Reduce time to first usable spec
- Improve clarity of problem framing
- Surface risks and edge cases earlier

## Approach
- Describe a rough product idea to Claude
- Ask Claude to ask clarifying questions
- Iterate until a clear problem statement and solution emerge

## Open Questions
- Where does AI help most: framing, ideation, critique, or synthesis?
- Where does it introduce risk or overconfidence?

## Next Steps
- Run 2–3 real experiments
- Compare AI‑assisted vs manual workflows
- Document learnings
